Hot chocolate

Ingredientes

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • “2 tablespoons of sugar (or to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • “A pinch of salt
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Grated chocolate (optional)

Preparation Method

  1. In a saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until it begins to boil. Stir occasionally to prevent the milk from sticking to the bottom of the pan.
  2. Add the cocoa powder, sugar, and salt to the hot milk. Stir well until the chocolate and sugar are completely dissolved
  3. Reduce the heat to medium-low and let the hot chocolate cook for another 2–3 minutes, stirring constantly. This will help slightly thicken the drink
  4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and add the vanilla extract. Stir well
  5. Pour the hot chocolate into cups or mugs. If desired, top with whipped cream for garnish and sprinkle with grated chocolate.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious hot chocolate!

Additional Information

Remember that you can adjust the amount of sugar to suit your personal taste. Additionally, you can also add other ingredients, such as ground cinnamon or marshmallows, to give your hot chocolate an extra touch.
