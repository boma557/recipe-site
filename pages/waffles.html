Waffles

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 3 beaten eggs
  • 4 tablespoons of melted unsalted butter
  • 1¾ cups of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract

Preparation Method

  1. Sift the all-purpose flour, sugar, cornstarch, baking powder, and salt into a bowl. Set aside.
  2. In another bowl, mix the beaten eggs with the milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract
  3. Pour over the flour mixture and quickly fold in the ingredients.
  4. Preheat the waffle maker. Pour a shallow ladle of batter and spread it to cover the waffle mold, close the lid, and cook until the batter is golden brown.
  5. Remove with silicone spatulas. Serve with honey, fruit, or jam.
