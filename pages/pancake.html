American pancake

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups of all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1¼ cups of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons of melted butter
  • Vegetable oil (for greasing the pan)

Preparation Method

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat the milk, egg, and melted butter until smooth.
  3. Pour the liquid mixture into the bowl with the dry ingredients. Gently stir with a spoon or whisk until the ingredients are just combined. Do not overmix, as this can make the pancakes heavy
  4. Preheat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Lightly grease the skillet with vegetable oil
  5. Use about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake. Pour the batter into the hot skillet and spread it slightly to form a round pancake.
  6. Cook until small bubbles appear on the surface of the pancake and the edges are lightly golden. This takes about 2–3 minutes.
  7. Flip the pancake with a spatula and cook for another 1–2 minutes, or until fully golden brown
  8. Transfer the pancake to a plate and repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  9. Serve the pancakes hot with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, fresh fruit, whipped cream, etc.”

Additional Information

This recipe makes approximately 8 medium pancakes. You can adjust the pancake size according to your preference. Enjoy your delicious American pancakes
